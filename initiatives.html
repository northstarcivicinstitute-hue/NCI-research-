Site rule

Not every idea needs to be a top-level brand.

PlayStrong and MSEEM sit at the top as flagship proof points. Navigate the Deal, MiYAEA, and the NIL Access Audit function as tools and policy vehicles. Governance lines live in the research library. Incubation concepts stay visible without crowding the portfolio.
