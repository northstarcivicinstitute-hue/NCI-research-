Site rule

Not every idea needs to be a top-level brand.

That is the main structural fix in this build. PlayStrong and MSEEM sit at the top. Program lines solve concrete audience problems. Governance lines stay in the library. Policy vehicles and future concepts stay visible without crowding the homepage.
