About NCI

Who We Are

A Michigan-based nonpartisan research and policy organization built on the belief that the communities who need good policy most are the ones with the least say in how it's designed.

Our Story

Northstar Civic Institute was founded in mid-Michigan by Donavan Norman, a public administrator and MPA candidate at Central Michigan University, out of a recognition that the data being produced by researchers and the tools being handed to practitioners were consistently failing the communities that needed them most.

Too often, rigorous research sits in academic journals while school districts make decisions without it. Policy tools are designed for large, well-resourced municipalities and never reach the rural county or the under-resourced urban district that would benefit most.

NCI was built to close these gaps. We produce nonpartisan research, pilot programs with independent evaluation, and design policy tools that are usable by the people actually on the ground.

"The communities that need policy the most are the ones who have the least say in how it's designed. NCI exists to change that."

- Donavan Norman, Founder & Executive Director

Our Values

What drives us

01 - NONPARTISAN
Evidence Without Agenda
We follow the data wherever it leads. Our research is designed to hold up under scrutiny from any political direction, and our policy tools are built to be useful regardless of who is in office.
02 - COMMUNITY-FIRST
Trust as Foundation
We operate in the communities we serve. Every program, every tool, and every brief starts with the question: does this work for the people who need it most?
03 - RIGOROUS
Proof, Not Promises
Independent evaluation is built into every program we pilot - not added after the fact. We don't claim success we can't demonstrate, and we publish what we learn even when outcomes are mixed.
04 - ACCESSIBLE
Research That Reaches People
Plain-language. Multilingual. District-ready. Our tools are designed to be used by a first-year school administrator without legal support - and scalable to a statewide system.
Our History

NCI Timeline

2025 - FOUNDING
NCI Incorporated in Michigan
Northstar Civic Institute is state incorporated in Michigan with an active EIN. Founder Donavan Norman begins building the organization's research and program infrastructure.
2025 - INITIATIVES
PlayStrong & MSEEM Launched
NCI launches its two core initiatives - PlayStrong, a youth sports and behavioral health pilot, and MSEEM, a statewide NIL accessibility audit responding to Michigan's extension of NIL rights to high school athletes.
2026 - RESEARCH
2026 Policy Brief Series Published
NCI publishes five evidence-based policy briefs under the series "Advancing Equity in the Athletic Economy," engaging policymakers across Michigan on stadium subsidies, NIL equity, workforce protection, youth access, and community benefits.
2026 - FEDERAL STATUS
501(c)(3) Application Pending
NCI's federal nonprofit application is in process, with Co-Founder Julie Bark serving as Chief Clinical Officer to lead behavioral health integration across PlayStrong's referral architecture.
