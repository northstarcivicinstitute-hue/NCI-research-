Who We Are

Built for the long game

Northstar Civic Institute was founded on the belief that the communities who need policy the most are the ones who have the least say in how it's designed. We change that — through rigorous research, direct program delivery, and tools that travel.

01
Nonpartisan Research
Evidence that holds up in any room — legislative, administrative, or community. We produce data that doesn't have a political agenda.
02

03
Policy Tools That Travel

Community Trust

PlayStrong

Youth Athletics + Social-Emotional Learning + Behavioral Health Referral Pathways

A six-month community pilot integrating coached athletics with SEL mentorship and clinical behavioral health referral pathways. Built for the youth who need it most — including those navigating food insecurity, housing instability, foster care, and justice involvement.

Statewide Audit — In Progress

MSEEM

Michigan Sports Equity & Economic Mobility

A nonpartisan audit mapping NIL and PBA accessibility gaps across Michigan's school districts — and producing district-ready compliance tools following Michigan's extension of NIL rights to high school athletes.

Legislative Context

The policy landscape

Three active Michigan bills shape the environment NCI is working in — and our tools directly inform their equitable implementation.

HB 4643

NIL Rights for High School Athletes

Active — House

Extends name, image, and likeness rights to Michigan high school student-athletes. NCI's MSEEM audit maps whether the equity infrastructure exists to make these rights real for under-resourced students.

HB 4371

Youth Sports Standards & Equity

Active — House

Establishes baseline standards for youth athletic programs. PlayStrong is an active, evidence-based model of exactly the kind of equitable youth sports programming this legislation contemplates.

SB 0014

Financial Access & Equity

Active — Senate

Addresses cashless payment equity — with direct implications for unbanked families navigating youth sports participation and NIL financial transactions.
